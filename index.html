Course policies in a nutshell

1. Course Duration & Structure : The total duration of the course is 6 months. The first 3 months will cover fundamentals of Python. The next 3 months will focus on real-time projects. We will guide students on how to apply for internships upon course completion.

2. Attendance & Absence Policy : Regular attendance is mandatory. If a student is absent for multiple days, we will not provide an e-certificate. Students must inform the tutor at least one week in advance for any planned leave.

3. Dropout Policy : No student is allowed to drop out once enrolled. If a student decides to drop out, they must pay the remaining course fee in full.

4. Certification Policy: We do not provide certificates during the course. Students who comply with attendance rules and have a good credit will receive an e-certificate upon completion.
